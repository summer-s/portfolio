Noell Honey

Noell Honey Brand Identity

Noell Honey is a family owned apiary, providing fresh and local honey to families in the area. They were just getting started, so I created a logo and poster to get them up and running. I thought up the slogan "from our hive to yours", to speak to the brand's family oriented ideals.
